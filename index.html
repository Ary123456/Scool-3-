WHAT'S NEW


  • Summer holidays are getting close!

    Students are excited for summer holidays. They can't wait for winter and spring to end. These summer holidays might be a normal summer holiday after the corona-virus infecting everyone's summer last year. We hope that corona-virus doesn't invade our summer this year.

  • Plovers invading our oval

    At the end of last year, we had one of the most terrifying events happening at our lunch time. Students saw birds on the oval so they went to look at them up close,They didn't know what would happen to them. The birds flew away then swooped the students. The students were not hurt. This was the 1st Plover attack. The reason they swooped us for a few weeks because they were trying to protect their chicks. In the present, their chicks have hatched which means they don't attack us also in other words we are kind of friends.
